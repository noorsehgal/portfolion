APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

Design concept for a mobile application & website that enables users to get easy access to sustainable fashion. The brand is called Heather. Its a vegan clothing brand that aims at sustainability & being ethical. The brand produces clothes that are unique. It does not follow fast fashion that keeps changing with each trend.Sustainable clothes are not just in style, but they are a very integral part of the future, hence the tagline: Not a trend, but the future. So basically the main objective of this brand is saving the planet but making it sustainable & fashionable in the process.
